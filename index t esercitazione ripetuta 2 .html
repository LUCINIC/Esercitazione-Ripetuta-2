Focaccia with caramelized Onion and, Brie.

Questo piatto è molto gustoso e sembra essere presentabile e di facilità estrema. Provare servito con della birra oppure del vino rosso.

Image: Sally Vegas

Feautered In :
Fresh Cranberry Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the Focaccia :


  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour

  • 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast

  • 1/2 teaspoons salt

  • 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water

  • 4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan


For the Topping :


  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced

  • tablespoon balsamic vinegar

  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top

  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a pairing kinfe if desired, though it's not necessary)

  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough

    In a medium-sized mixing bow, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachement, mix together all the dough ingredients until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough,
    though I sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incirporate the last of the flour...
